Boossara

"Best song of 2014" - VNsNow

Boossara’s haunting vocal performances and sultry orchestral ballads have captured the interest of entertainment publishers, critics and fans alike. Her music has been featured in numerous video games, commercials and new media since 2013. One of the most prolific singers within the visual novel game genre, Boossara’s vocals have graced nearly 10 video game theme songs including "Seasons Of The Wolf", "Cursed Lands", and "Planet Stronghold 2". Anyone with a passion for fantasy, science fiction, mystery and imagination will be intrigued by her music.
